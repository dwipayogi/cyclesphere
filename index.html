Category

Road Bikes

Designed for speed and distance on paved roads, road bikes are perfect for long-distance riders, speed enthusiasts, and commuters. They have lightweight frames, thin tires, and handlebars that curve under for a streamlined riding position.

Mountain Bikes

Built for rugged trails and off-road riding, mountain bikes have strong frames, wide tires, and high-traction tread for stability and control on rough terrain. They also feature shock-absorbing suspension systems for comfort during adventurous rides.

Hybrid Bikes

Combining the best of road and mountain bikes, hybrid bikes are versatile options for city riding, light trail riding, or commuting. They have medium-width tires, a comfortable upright riding position, and often include cargo racks or fenders.
